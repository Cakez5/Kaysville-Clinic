Our Mission

At Kaysville Clinic, our mission is to empower individuals to achieve lasting health and vitality through personalized, evidence-based care. We address all aspects of wellness—including weight management, balanced nutrition, and hormone replacement therapy—while also providing comprehensive support for chronic disease, illness, injury recovery, and opioid dependence. Through compassionate, integrated care, we guide our patients toward improved quality of life, sustainable wellness, and long-term healing of both body and mind.

Our Team

Dr. Layne Kamalu, M.D.

Medical Doctor

Dr. Kamalu was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. After spending his childhood and teen years in Hawaii, Arizona and Germany, he served an LDS mission to Munich, Germany and graduated from Brigham Young University in Mechanical Engineering. Dr. Kamalu attended medical school at St. Louis University School of Medicine in St. Louis, Missouri. He completed a residency in Family Practice at Scott Medical Center, Scott AFB in Illinois. He is Board Certified by the American Board of Family Practice.

Cara Mellor, NP

Nurse Practitioner

Cara Mellor was born and raised in Northern Utah. She attended College at Weber State University where she graduated with her nursing degree in 2005. In 2007 she completed her NP (Nurse Practitioner) degree and started practicing in Ogden, Utah. In 2019 Cara Mellor joined Kaysville Clinic and sees patients part-time. She is married and has 2 daughters. She enjoys time with family, camping and hiking.

Abi McCloskey

Master Aesthetician
