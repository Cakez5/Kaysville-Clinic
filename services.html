Primary Care

Your trusted partner for ongoing health needs, annual exams, and personalized care.

Comprehensive family medicine

We provide same-day support for acute illness, chronic condition management, preventive care, and long-term wellness planning.

  • Acute illness treatment for colds, flu, sinus issues, ear infections, and more
  • Chronic condition care for diabetes, blood pressure, asthma, and other long-term concerns
  • Annual physicals, sports physicals, scout physicals, missionary physicals, and FAA exams
  • Women’s health, men’s health, and mental health support
  • Immunizations and routine screenings

Minor Surgery

Comfortable, professional treatment for common minor procedures and outpatient care.

Procedures we perform

  • Skin biopsies, mole removal, wart removal, cyst removal, and lipoma removal
  • Ingrown toenail treatment including partial or full nail avulsion when needed
  • Laceration repair and wound care with attention to scarring and healing
  • Injury evaluation for strains, sprains, fractures, and other common concerns

Wellness & Prevention

Preventive screenings, lifestyle guidance, and proactive care to keep you healthy.

Prevention-focused care

  • Routine wellness visits and health coaching
  • Tetanus and diphtheria immunizations
  • Flu shots and seasonal preventive care
  • Screenings for women’s and men’s health concerns
  • Education around diet, sleep, movement, and long-term healthy habits

Weight Management

Supportive plans and guidance for healthy, sustainable weight goals.

Medication-assisted weight loss

We offer evidence-based GLP-1 programs with semaglutide and tirzepitide for patients who need medical support with weight loss and body composition goals.

  • Medical evaluation and individualized treatment plans
  • Guidance on nutrition, sleep, exercise, and stress management
  • Monitoring of progress, side effects, and long-term success
  • Support for patients who want to lose weight and keep it off

Hormone Therapy

Personalized hormone care to help support energy, balance, and overall wellness.

Hormone and testosterone replacement

We help patients address symptoms related to menopause, low testosterone, fatigue, low libido, and age-related hormone decline.

  • Hormone replacement therapy for menopause symptoms
  • Testosterone replacement therapy for men experiencing fatigue, low energy, or reduced vitality
  • Bioidentical and conventional hormone options
  • Customized treatment plans with follow-up and safety monitoring

Addiction Management

Compassionate support and treatment planning for recovery and long-term wellness.

Recovery-focused care

  • Medication-assisted treatment for opioid dependence
  • Support for substance use disorders and alcoholism
  • Therapy, counseling, and individualized recovery planning
  • Compassionate care for patients navigating dependence and withdrawal symptoms

Aesthetics

Skin-focused services designed to help you look and feel your best.

Laser and skin rejuvenation

  • ClearLift by Harmony XL Pro for fine lines, wrinkles, sagging skin, tone, texture, pores, sun damage, acne scars, and skin tightening
  • Dye VL for pigmented lesions, sunspots, age spots, freckles, melasma, and birthmarks
  • Laser hair removal with Harmony XL Pro SHR for long-lasting, comfortable hair reduction
  • Botox for cosmetic smoothing as well as medical treatment of migraines, sweating, and muscle tension
  • Sclerotherapy for varicose and spider veins
